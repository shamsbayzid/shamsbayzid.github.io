I am a an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology. I completed my PhD in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Austin ans was supervised by Prof. Tandy Warnow and Prof. Joydeep Ghosh.

My primary research interests lie in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. I am interested in developing novel algorithmic approaches to solve different discrete optimization problems on species tree estimation, considering gene duplications and losses, deep coalescences, horizontal gene transfers etc. I also work on computational proteomics and interested in applications of machine learning in computational biology.
